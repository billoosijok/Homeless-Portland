PDX Can Do Better

At the most basic level, we believe people should have a warm and dry place to sleep, access to services they need and laws enforced so that everyone is safe.

Janus Youth Programs

We change the lives of 6,000 youth every year through our 40 programs in Oregon and Washington. Youth who have runaway or are homeless. Teen parents and youth living in low-income neighborhoods.

Portland Rescue Mission

The Portland Rescue Mission has had a tireless commitment to breaking the cycle of homelessness, addiction and despair in the lives of hurting people in need.

Human Solutions

At Human Solutions, our vision is to foster a prosperous and healthy community free of homelessness and poverty.

Share House of Vancouver

Share’s mission is to lead the hungry and homeless to self-sufficiency by providing food, shelter, housing, education, advocacy and compassion through the strength of our community.

Open House Ministries

More than a shelter: Welcome to Open House Ministries, a family shelter serving homeless families and children in need in the Vancouver, WA area.

Winter Hospitality Overflow

We’re the Winter Hospitality Overflow, a Vancouver, WA-based organization devoted to helping those in need during winter’s coldest months.

Council For The Homeless

We invite you to sign up for our Housing Advocacy emails and stay informed on housing and homelessness solutions and opportunities. Let’s mobilize and create solutions at the local and state level.
